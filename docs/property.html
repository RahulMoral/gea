Description

The quintessential Edwardian home located in this prestigious address in the South Ward area of the town overlooking the golf course. The vendors have found a suitable property that they wish to purchase.

A particularly fine period home bursting with character and period features occupying a level position in the favoured South Ward area of the town. This area has arguably some of the finest residential properties, most of which are individual. This handsome property overlooks Weston Golf course to the front and you are, of course, moments from the beach. You can walk into the town centre in 15 minutes and the train station in 20. The current vendors have maintained the property very well and we would strongly advise viewing to appreciate this special home.

An attractive and quite ornate timber porch/conservatory provides you with a great welcome. This has attractive stained glass features and a ceramic tiled floor. A stained glass door enters into an impressive reception hallway where the star of the show is the central twin balustrade staircase which ascends to the first floor. To the front of the property there is a huge Sitting Room which has a feature fireplace as the main focal point.

This used to be 2 rooms and has been opened up to form one very large one. This can easily be separated, if required. There is a large walk in bay window to the front in addition to further windows to both front and side aspects. Off the hall you will find a generously sized Cloakroom with low level wc & sink with cupboard under. To the rear of the property you will find the Dining Room and the Kitchen/Breakfast Room.
